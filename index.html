Who We Are

Ummahpreneurs Mauritius is a dynamic community of entrepreneurs, innovators, and business leaders committed to fostering growth, collaboration, and success. We believe in the power of collective wisdom and shared experiences to transform ideas into thriving businesses.

Through networking events, workshops, mentorship programs, and business competitions, we create opportunities for entrepreneurs to learn, connect, and scale their ventures. Our mission is to build a supportive ecosystem where ambition meets opportunity.
