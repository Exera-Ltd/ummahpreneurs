Who We Are

Ummahpreneurs Mauritius is a dynamic community of entrepreneurs, innovators, and business leaders committed to fostering growth, collaboration, and success. We believe in the power of collective wisdom and shared experiences to transform ideas into thriving businesses.

Through networking events, workshops, mentorship programs, and business competitions, we create opportunities for entrepreneurs to learn, connect, and scale their ventures. Our mission is to build a supportive ecosystem where ambition meets opportunity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Ummahpreneurs?

Ummahpreneurs is open to all Muslim entrepreneurs in Mauritius, whether you're just starting with an idea or already running a business. We welcome founders, investors, and mentors who align with our vision of ethical, faith-driven entrepreneurship.

What does the pre-incubation program involve?

Our 7-week pre-incubation program includes workshops on business fundamentals, Islamic finance, mentorship sessions, peer networking, and culminates in a pitch day where you present your refined business idea to judges and potential investors.

Is there a fee to join?

We strive to keep our programs accessible. Some events may have nominal fees to cover costs, but we offer scholarships for those in financial need. Contact us to discuss your situation.

How do I apply for the next cohort?

Click the "Bismillah Join our community" button and select "I'm a Founder" to access the application form. Applications for Cohort 2026 are now open!

What makes Ummahpreneurs different?

We integrate Islamic principles into every aspect of business development. From halal financing options to ethical business practices, we ensure your entrepreneurial journey aligns with your faith while achieving commercial success.
